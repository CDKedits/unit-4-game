Welcome to the Crystal Collector!

You'll be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal, you'll be adding a specific amount of points to your total score.

To win, you have to match your total score to the random number displayed. You lose the game if your total score goes above that number.

You won't know the value of each crystal until you click on them.

The values for these crystals change at the start of each new game.
